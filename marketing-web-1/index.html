Want to Organize a Potluck?

Potlucks are always a great, inexpensive way to bring people together. They take the pressure off one person having to do everything and allow everyone to contribute their favorite dishes with friends, family or co-workers.

And while potlucks take the pressure off a single party planner, they still require some coordination to make them a success. After all, who wants to attend a party where there are 20 different versions of green beans?

Using this site/app will make the organization of your next potluck a breeze! By having party-goers RSVP and sign up for what they will bring online, you no longer have to worry about having too much broccoli and not enough chocolate!
